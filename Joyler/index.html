The Joyler Project

This here's a little diddly-doo for The Euler Project. The HTML here is mostly just a shell for the Javascript. A lot of the base styling is ripped from The Euler Project site itself and then customized to specs I like.

Problem 1:
Find the sum of all the multiples of 3 or 5 below 1000.

Problem 2:
By considering the terms in the Fibonacci sequence whose values do not exceed four million, find the sum of the even-valued terms.

Problem 3:
What is the largest prime factor of the number 600851475143 ?
